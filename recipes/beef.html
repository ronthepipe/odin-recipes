Roasted Beef

A tasty roasted beef recipe made by my mother.

This is how to Cook Melt In Your Mouth Roast Beef (easy recipe).

Ingredients

  • 4 lb organic beef roast
  • 2 Tbsp fine sea salt
  • 2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 Tbsp grass-fed butter, ghee, or avocado oil
  • ½ cup organic beef broth
  • ½ cup dry red wine
  • 3 sprigs rosemary
  • 1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Place it in small bowl combine salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat oven to 275ºF.
  3. Melt butter and put it on the piece of meat.
  4. Place roast beef in pan with all the ingredients.
  5. Oven roast at 275ºF until you get to 115ºF.
  6. Put to rest 5min and serve!
