Authentic Canadian Poutine Recipe

Easy at home poutine without going at bell province! (yikes)

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 6 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup unbleached all purpose flou
  • 20 oz beef broth
  • 10 oz chicken broth
  • Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare the gravy: In a small bowl, dissolve the cornstarch in the water and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter. Add the flour and cook, stirring regularly, for about 5 minutes, until the mixture turns golden brown
  3. Add the beef and chicken broth and bring to a boil
  4. Remove the potatoes from the water and place onto a sheet of paper towel. Blot to remove as much excess moisture as possible
  5. Add your fries to the 300Â°F oil and cook for 5-8 minutes, just until potatoes are starting to cook but are not yet browned.
  6. Add the cheese curds and toss with the hot fries and gravy.
